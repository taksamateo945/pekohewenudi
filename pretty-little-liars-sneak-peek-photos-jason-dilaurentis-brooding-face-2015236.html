    Pretty Little Liars Sneak Peek Photos: Jason DiLaurentis Is Brooding

    Us Weekly and Pretty Little Liars have partnered up again to bring you a sneak peek at each weeks new episode with behind-the-scenes photos. Scroll down for more on this weeks pics, and check back every Tuesday morning to see new ones!

    11 May 2024, 00:00

    Us Weekly and Pretty Little Liars have partnered up again to bring you a sneak peek at each week’s new episode with behind-the-scenes photos. Scroll down for more on this week’s pics, and check back every Tuesday morning to see new ones!

    Oh, Jason, let Us give you a hug!

    So long as last week’s final scene wasn’t a total fake-out, then tonight’s Pretty Little Liars episode (“Don’t Look Now”) will likely center around the fact that Jason’s (Drew Van Acker) “imaginary” childhood friend was a little more on the real side. And how is he dealing, you might ask? Just look at those bitten lips.

    “Jason is genuinely hit hard by the realization that his ‘imaginary friend’ was actually a real person,” executive producer I. Marlene King told Us Weekly. “His parents lied to him all this time, and Jason eventually believed their story. ‘Charlie wasn’t a real person. He was your imaginary friend.’ Now we can understand why Jason has been so troubled in the show.”

    Truer words! But of course, he isn’t the series’ only tortured soul. While all of the Liars have suffered at the hands of A, Aria’s (Lucy Hale) heart seems to have been hit the hardest, as she’s been handed at least two shady love interests: Ezra (Ian Harding) and Andrew (Brandon Jones).

    “Aria started the show as a ‘romantic,’ and in spite of all she has been through, she still believes in the possibility of true love,” King dished. “I wouldn’t want to live in a world where Aria stops believing in love.”
