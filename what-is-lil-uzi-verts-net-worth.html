    PHILADELPHIA rapper Lil Uzi Vert has never been one to take the regular route.

    Fans of the hitmaker were first shocked when the rapper got a diamond implanted into their forehead back in February 2021.

    What is Lil Uzi Vert’s net worth?

    Lil Uzi Vert, 26, is thought to have a net worth of $25milllion, according to Celebrity Net Worth.

    Between September 2017 and September 2018 they are said to have earned $20m, largely thanks to streaming and their live shows.

    In early 2021, the rapper spent $24million on getting a pink diamond inserted into their forehead.

    In July 2021, on their continued course of extravagant spontaneity, it was reported that Uzi was in the process of purchasing a planet.

    The rapper’s close friend Grimes debuted the shocking news.

    “Apparently @LILUZIVERT owns this planet – just a heads up,” Grimes wrote on Twitter, responding to a photo of a planet called WASP-127b.

    Discovered by NASA in 2016, the exoplanet is 1.4 times the size of Jupiter.

    “I tried 2 surprise everyone,” Vert replied to her. “Still working on it.” 

    Who is Lil Uzi Vert?

    Lil Uzi Vert is an American rapper and singer who goes by the pronouns: they/them, according to his Instagram bio.

    Born on July 31, 1994, their real name is Symere Bysil Woods.

    The rapper released their first project, the EP Purple Thoughtz Vol 1 in 2014, which brought them to the attention of ASAP Mob and producer Don Cannon.

    Lil Uzi released their first studio album Luv Is Rage 2 in August 2017 which contained the single XO Tour Llif3.

    The album debuted at number one on the Billboard chart.

    Who is Lil Uzi Vert dating?

    Jatavia “JT” Shakara Johnson

    Lil Uzi Vert was rumored to be dating JT from the Florida female rap duo, The City Girls.

    Jatavia “JT” Shakara Johnson has taken to Twitter on a few occasions to speak about, or directly to, Lil Uzi.

    In November 2020, Lil Uzi posted a throwback photo of the pair, which fans believed to be a clear indicator of a romantic relationship.

    In April 2022, Lil Uzi tweeted that JT had apparently broken up with Uzi: “Jt broke up with me over this dumb ass shit. I don’t even do nothin FML.”

    After deleting the tweet about the breakup, Uzi tweeted again writing: “So lonely I wonder if you would notice if I didn’t exist …Just thoughts from Uzi.”

    In May, Uzi tweeted, “Trust me you will be back they always come back.”

    The pair’s relationship status is currently unknown.

    Dream Doll

    Lil Uzi was rumored to have dated reality star and Instagram model turned rapper Dream Doll.

    The two were allegedly together from 2017-2018.

    On National Boyfriend Day in 2017, Dream Doll appeared to confirm the relationship after she uploaded a video of Lil Uzi.

    Brittany Renner

    Instagram model Brittany Renner released a tell-all book called Judge This Cover in 2018, detailing her relationships with different men in the industry.

    Lil Uzi is said to be mentioned in the book.

    According to reports, Brittany once took to Instagram to accuse the rapper of cheating on her.

    “I’ve given you so many chances after embarrassing me publicly time and time again when I know you still f*** with that other b**** — on top of the other one and God knows how many more,” she said according to The Jasmine Brand.
