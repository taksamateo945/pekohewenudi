    It seems as if Arnold Schwarzenegger has found a new hobby, and he’s pretty darn good at it. Schwarzenegger is trying his hand at disguising himself – no idea how it’s working. In his latest attempt, Schwarzenegger disguises himself as “Howard’, a trainer-turned regional manager at Gold’s Gym in Venice Beach, California. Howard offers some advice and motivation to some unknowing gym members.

    He shared the video yesterday during his second Reddit AMA. The video is an effort to raise donations for his After-School All-Stars program in Los Angeles, which he founded in 2002 to provide productive and healthy extracurricular opportunities for students.

    When it comes to Arnie disguising himself, we think he’s found his new calling – “I have noticed lately people love to put me in disguises,” Arnold told commenters. “You obviously saw the Bjorn Borg look. But I also made this video with Reddit in mind, so I might as well put it out now.” I think we can expect to see him in plenty more going forward.
