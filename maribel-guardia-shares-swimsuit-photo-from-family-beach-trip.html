    Maribel Guardia first rose to fame when she won Miss Costa Rica in 1978. She then began a career in the entertainment industry. Guardia starred in shows like Tu y yo and Una familia con suerte. Guardia is also a singer and TV host. She's currently enjoying a vacation in the Bahamas, and shared a set of swimsuit photos on Instagram. Guardia captioned the post, "AND THE BEAUTIFUL OF THIS IS LIFE. COSER SUENOS, BORDAR STORIES AND POWER TO UNLEASH. THE KNOTS OF OUR DAYS." How does she stay so fit? Read on to see 5 ways Maribel Guardia stays in shape and the photos that prove they work.

    Guardia is an avid dancer. She shares a lot of posts on Instagram of herself dancing. Guardia shared this video of herself dancing on stage. Prudential reports that dance has a lot of health benefits. "The movements associated with dance can increase flexibility and reduce stiffness.     Guardia makes sure to eat healthy, and avoids unhealthy foods as much as she can. She shared some of her secrets in an interview. One of Guardia's secrets is carrying healthy options with her. "I try to carry healthy things in the bag, so I don't have to eat filthy things like chips, candy and so on."

    Guardia is a big believer in balance when it comes to her diet. Especially when it comes to carbohydrates. In the same interview, Guardia says that she limits carbs during most of the week, and has one day where she indulges. "When I eat a tortilla in the morning, for the rest of the day I don't eat another one and I avoid bread," she explained. "On Sundays I eat everything: pizza, hamburgers, desserts."

    Guardia likes to spend time outside. One thing she likes to do is go on hikes. She shared these photos and videos on Instagram of herself hiking in Costa Rica.
