    Draya Michele is making plans for baby number two. The celebrity mom shared pictures of herself while showing off her bump a few days ago.

    “I am a woman….. I’m creating life…. I’m proud and I’m happy,” she captioned underneath one photo of herself in the nude. “I don’t need materialistic things or the love and acceptance from anyone, now that I have you. I love you son.” Michele later edited the post to reflect both of her kids.

    “I’ve been hurt, abandoned, lied on, and disrespected,” the star wrote. “I’ve realized you can’t change people.. And the thing about rough times is, I’ve survived every single one.”

    She went on to celebrate her expectancy saying, “Today is a celebration for me …. I’ve made 6 months…. And I’m ok rejoicing alone. I have a son and another on the way, soon to be surrounded by 2 kings. Protectors.”

    Draya has been concealing her bump well these past few months. The celebrity mom was in attendance at Tank’s party a few days ago, and wore a black leather dress to the Golden Globe Awards that covered her protruding stomach well.

    This will be Draya’s first child with Orlando Scandrick. The celebrity mom also has a son named Kniko.
