    A well-known online personality, Freckled Zelda is active on TikTok, Instagram, and YouTube.

    She publishes cosplays and gives concerts in front of captivated crowds all around the world in her fairy persona. Her videos typically feature vivacious and upbeat ocarina solos. Her aesthetic could be described as joyful, humorous, and whimsical.

    On the ninth episode of America’s Got Talent’s seventeenth season, which aired in prime time on the American broadcast television network NBC, Freckled Zelda made her official television debut in front of an even larger audience.

    Who Is Rachael Wilson Aka Freckled Zelda From AGT?

    Freckled Zelda

    Online publications by Rachael Wilson are made under the pen name Freckled Zelda.

    As a “music fairy” from the “freckled woodland,” Zelda presented herself. She made a point of displaying her jewellery. Ocarina, a wind instrument, was slung around Zelda’s neck.

    The judges were also told by freckled Zelda that the ocarina is a sweet potato flute. With the help of an ocarina and a rendition of the song “Colors of the Wind,” freckled Zelda managed to win over the first dubious judges.

    All four judges gave Freckled Zelda a “yes” when she performed the song’s original rendition in the Disney movie “Pocahontas,” which was sung by Judy Kuhn.

    On her YouTube channel, Freckled Zelda posts covers of songs that she has played the ocarina to. She also posts videos of her giving performances in public and going to conventions.

    You may watch her convention videos on her TikTok account as well. In numerous of her films, Zelda ambushes cosplayers while playing the ocarina in the background until they glance up and notice her.

    In addition to performing covers, Zelda also sings her original songs, some of which are available on Spotify. Her most recent hit, “I Feel Rather Sad,” features both the ocarina and the guitar, which are frequently used in her original compositions.

    Freckled Many of Zelda’s original songs use both of them. Additionally, those who admire her can buy the ocarinas and colourful contact lenses that she wears. These can be purchased from particular merchants listed on her Instagram feed. She also has a storefront for products.

    Freckled Zelda Is A TikTok Star

    On the TikTok app, Freckled Zelda has more than 7 million followers, and her content has received more than 170 million likes.

    Almost usually, her videos receive more than one million views. By sharing the clip on TikTok, she teased her admirers with a snippet from her impending appearance on America’s Got Talent. Zelda has published multiple videos on her appearance on America’s Got Talent and her participation in it.

    Zelda discloses in one of the videos that she will be dressing as a fairy on the America’s Got Talent set and that the production crew has never previously used a music fairy.

    Freckled Zelda posted a brief video on her TikTok account in response to a complaint that she wears too much makeup. She walked through how she does makeup in this video.

    She uses eye contacts, false eyelashes, and lip liner to create her distinctive fairy appearance, along with fake ears and the occasional application of concealer. She additionally dons fake eyelids.

    Videos of Freckled Zelda performing backstage at “America’s Got Talent” can also be found on her TikTok account. These films demonstrate how thrilled she is to participate in the show.

    Zelda also broadcasts videos in which she is joined by other cosplayers. In the videos, Zelda can be seen playing music for the other characters to see how they respond to it.

    Freckled Zelda Is A TikTok Star

    With more than 300k followers on Instagram and 7 million on TikTok, Freckled Zelda is a phenomenon on the internet.

    We don’t know a lot about Zelda’s parents or family history, despite the fact that we are aware of her extreme enthusiasm for music and her ability to shift into a fairy. There is no information available on Zelda because she keeps her personal life private.
