    Garcelle Beauvais’ son, Oliver Saunders, is a proud father. The young dad recently took to Instagram to celebrate his status as a parent and to also promise his son, Oliver Saunders Jr.

    https://www.instagram.com/p/CAWm0DcnLRH/

    “Everything I do is for you, son,” Oliver wrote. “I will always be there for you,” the young father promised. “I will always love you,” big Oliver added.

    Garcelle Beauvais got candid about her oldest son’s struggle with drug addiction during a recent episode of the Real Housewives of Beverly Hills (RHOBH). “My oldest son, Oliver, who is doing great now,” Garcelle shared during her reflection segment.

    “I got married really young, and then Oliver came,” Garcelle explained. “He was the light of both of our eyes and then he started hanging out with the wrong crowd [and] got into drugs. We did everything that money could offer him. It was really, really difficult to watch him sort of unravel.”

    Oliver Saunders made it through his struggles thanks to his support system. Now, he is a father. “It’s crazy how time flies,” Garcelle’s oldest son recently shared online. “Mommy, daddy, and our family love you more than you will ever know,” Oliver said.
